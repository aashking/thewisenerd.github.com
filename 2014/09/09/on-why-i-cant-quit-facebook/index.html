on why i can't quit facebook?

Published September 9th 2014

Facebook, the world's largest SNS(Social Networking Site), with 1 of 3 persons on Facebook (or so is advertised), does a lot of bad things. I ain't here to talk about those. If you would like to know, you can read them here, but I am here to more talk on why I can't quit it.

  • All my friends (and frenemies) are there! (Not a very good reason, but the rest of the points do make sense).
  • Some of the important anouncements like homeworks to be done, projects to be submitted, etc, etc are usually shared on it (I hate this.)
  • some of my old friends are there, and that's the only way I have contact with them, so, it's a no go.
  • Lastly, I need an account in case I need to quickly contact them (since my phone's mostly (read always) "clean" (I'm a ROM builder, droid stuff you aren't likely to understand), this is essential.

Given those, on why I can't quit facebook, I'd take the opportunity to say that you can follow me on Twitter. It isn't noisy, and I can work in peace.
